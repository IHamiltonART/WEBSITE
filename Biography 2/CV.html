Ian Hamilton                                                                                                     Biography


      


      

Timelines
1974: Graduated from S.A. School of Art. Adelaide. SA.
1975: Awarded Australia Council Living Artist Fellowship.
1976: Artist in Residence, Griffith University, Brisbane. Qld.
1978: Awarded South Australian arts grant to study the Art of the Golden Bowerbird
1983: Published The Ceremony of the Golden Bowerbird: Playground for Paranoids, 
           Experimental Art Foundation, Adelaide. SA.
1983 - 1996: Arts Administration. Prospect Council, Adelaide, SA.
1995: Researches book on Country Australia.
1996: Heart of the Country published by Wakefield Press 1996.
1996 - 2001: Arts Manager, Mildura Rural City Council. Vic.
2001 - Freelance artist, Adelaide. SA.
